Shan biryani masala

Ingredients requireed

  • Meat on bones 1kg /2.2lbs
  • Rice, basmati 3 1/2 cups/750g,
  • Onions 3 medium /300g,
  • Tomatoes 3-4 medium /300g,
  • Potatoes 2 medium /250,
  • Garlic paste 2 table spoons
  • Ginger paste 2 table spoons
  • Yogurt, paste 1 cup /200g,
  • cooking oil 1 cup /175 ml
  • Shan special bombay biryani masala 1 packet, mix in 1/2 cup water

STEPS OF COOKING

  1. Fry onions in hot until golden. add tomatoes and fry until oil sparates
  2. Add mear, garlic paste, ginger paste, yogurt, potatoes and shan masala bombay biryani masala. stir fry for 10 mintues add water (beef/lamb 4 cups, chicken 2 cups). cover and cook on low heat until meat is tender. then increase heat and stir fry until oil seprates from gravy.
  3. separately in 15 cups /3 table spoons of shan salt and soaked rice boil rice util 3/4 cooked. remove and darin thoroughly.
  4. spread half. rice in pot and pour meat curry. top with remaining rice. cover pot and cook on law heat until ricecuts. is fully cooked (5-10 mintues.) mix before serving TIPS for meat, use breast, ribs & shoulder cuts.
